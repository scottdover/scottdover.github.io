The folks at C.R. Law were truly a joy to work with. My fondest memory was a photo of one of the attorneys playing an air guitar. It's a shame that didn't make it to production.

Like many of the sites I've worked with, this project combined CodeIgniter and Wordpress that made it simple to both blog and manage details of the firm, like practice areas and their relationships to attorneys.

One of my favorite pieces of this site is the people page. We took a small jQuery plugin and turned it into something that is somewhat atypical for a law firm of their size. It was a pleasure to work with the C.R. Law team and a nice reminder that some attorneys are just fun.
