The Law Offices of R. Scott Dover is a single page website for an attorney in my hometown, Pickens, SC.

This project was important to me in a couple of ways. First, the attorney is my uncle with whom I share my name. Second, I had the opportunity to go back for a visit and take some photographs. The photos in the slideshow are ones I took from around the Pickens area. The picture below was taken just a mile from my childhood home.

This project was a great chance to help out a local business, reconnect with a high school classmate (now a partner at the firm), and work on developing my responsive design skills in a way that was enjoyable.

As a side note, this project was created using the Slim, a PHP framework developed by a company here in the state of North Carolina.
