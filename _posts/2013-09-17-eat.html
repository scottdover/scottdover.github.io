I'm definitely eating more sugary, empty calories and am going to get back on track. Luckily, I've gained very little weight, and I want to keep it that way. That's why I'm keeping a log of everything I eat here. Follow along and by all means call me out if it doesn't get better. Truth is, I don't think I'm the only one that is completely unaware of how much I eat in a day.

It all starts with being mindful.
