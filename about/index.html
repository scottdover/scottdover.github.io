About Me

I'm a father, a husband, and keeper of the faith (I'm a Christian). I used to say I enjoy coffee, but feel my enjoyment has slowly turned to an addiction. I'm a remote web developer, and wouldn't have it any other way.

My wife, Nicole, and I have a beautiful 7 month old, Maddie. As a result, life's certainly become a lot more busy. In our free time, we enjoy kayaking, hiking, and bowling. And I play a mean “Smoke On The Water” on the six-string.

Being a creator of the web, I love the possibilities technology offers. But I definitely appreciate the moments in life that take you away from a screen. Let's just say I enjoy my instagram filters as much as the scene behind them.
